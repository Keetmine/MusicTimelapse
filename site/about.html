About Project

"Music Timelapse" is a musical interactive experience based on sampling one or several of the most popular songs a year in the period from 1988 to 2012 in the genre Indie Rock.
Each year includes a whole or part of the song, a description of the group and songs and a mini game that works with the song.

"Music Timelapse" is a final project for the course "Developing Web Applications in JavaScript"
